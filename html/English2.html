  • High-density Polyethylene is a thermoplastic made up of ethylene monomers

How to get rid:


  • Clean excess food or drain out any liquid.

  • Place the item in your curbside recycling bin or take to a container deposit depot.

  • Do not put soft HDPE plastics into your recycling bin.

  • If you are not able to contribute to recycling it, throw it in garbage bins or containers in your area to make sure it goes to a designated place.

  • Do not throw it in the street, public places or cultivated areas.

  • Do not throw it in rivers, canals, seas or oceans; Because that would pollute the water and harm marine creatures.

Recycling


  • Recycle logo plastic #2 means that it can be easily and efficiently recycled up to 10 times.

  • Clear containers from HDPE 2 plastic are recycled back into the same new containers.

    • Colored HDPE 2 is turned into many other items like:
    • pipes, lumber,and toys.
    • lawn, pens, and floor tiles.

  • It is a relatively simple and cost-effective process to recycle HDPE plastic for secondary use.

    • It can be Recycled into:
    • Laundry detergent bottles,and oil bottles.
    • Pens, recycling containers, and floor tile.
    • Drainage pipe, lumber, and benches.
    • Doghouses, picnic tables, fencing, or shampoo bottles.

safety:


  • One of the safest forms of plastic.

  • HDPE has good chemical resistance meaning it will not react with foods, beverages, or household chemicals being stored


  • HDPE plastic is very hard-wearing and does not break down under exposure to sunlight or extremes of heating or freezing

    • The durability and reliability of HDPE 2 make it efficient to use in the manufacture of various items like:
    • Sturdy bottles for cosmetics and household cleaner
    • Stools and chairs.
    • sunbeds for outdoor use , Toys and playground equipment
    • Some plastic bags , Flexible pipes , Bottle crates , and Rope.
    • Plastic envelopes , Water, juice and milk jugs

  • It s also the preferred material for electrical insulation, and both HDPE and LDPE are used for this purpose.
