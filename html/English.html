How to get rid:


  • When you finish using the product, you should seek to recycle that product by sorting it at home or in the sorting places outside the house.

  • If you are not able to contribute to recycling it, throw it in garbage bins or containers in your area to make sure it goes to a designated place.

  • Do not throw it in rivers, canals, seas or oceans; Because that would pollute the water and harm marine creatures; As it is difficult to decompose, the time required for the decomposition of a water bottle may reach more than four centuries

Recycling


  • It is accepted in recycling plants because it is easy to recycle.
